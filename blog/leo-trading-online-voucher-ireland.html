How to Get the LEO Trading Online Voucher for Your Irish Business

A
Avenor Team
Published • July 2026
TL;DR The Local Enterprise Office (LEO) in Ireland offers a €2,500 grant (the Trading Online Voucher) to help small businesses build or upgrade their e-commerce capabilities. You must have fewer than 10 employees, be trading for 6 months, and attend a mandatory seminar. Avenor can provide the necessary quotes and build your compliant e-commerce site.

If you own a small business in Ireland and want to start selling your products or services online, the financial cost of a high-quality e-commerce website can seem daunting. Fortunately, the Irish government provides incredible support through the Local Enterprise Office (LEO).

The Trading Online Voucher Scheme offers financial assistance of up to €2,500 with co-funding of 50% from the business. Here is your step-by-step guide to claiming it in 2026.

1. Are You Eligible?

Before applying, you must meet the strict eligibility criteria set by LEO. Your business must:

  • Have no more than 10 employees.
  • Have less than €2 million in turnover.
  • Be trading for at least 6 months.
  • Be located in the area covered by the specific LEO to whom you make the application.

2. The Application Process

Step 1: Attend the Information Seminar

You cannot simply fill out a form online and receive the funds. You are required to attend a Trading Online Voucher information seminar hosted by your local LEO. These are currently held online and provide excellent insights into what is required.

Step 2: Get Three Quotes

LEO requires you to submit three distinct quotes from web development agencies. This is to ensure you are getting fair market value. The quotes must detail the breakdown of costs (e.g., design, development, SEO, payment gateway integration).

Note: At Avenor, we frequently provide detailed, LEO-compliant quotes for Irish businesses seeking this voucher.

Step 3: Submit Your Application

Along with the quotes, you must submit a detailed proposal explaining how an e-commerce website will help your business grow. You need to outline your target market, your expected ROI, and your digital marketing strategy.

3. What Can the Voucher Be Used For?

The grant is designed to help you trade online, not just build an "online brochure." Permitted expenses include:

  • IT consultation and web development.
  • Development or upgrade of an e-commerce website.
  • Implementing online payments or booking systems.
  • Purchase of internet-related software.
  • Digital marketing strategy and execution (up to 30% of approved voucher costs).
